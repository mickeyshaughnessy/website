  • Large Scale Atomistic Simulation of Carbon Nanotube Contacts. The dynamics of individual atoms and the electrical contact resistance are computed using density functional theory and molecular dynamics on large-scale supercomputers. In this simulation, a carbon nanotube (purple atoms) is viewed head-on while resting on a silicon substrate surface (light blue atoms). Palladium and titanium atoms (silver and light green) form a metallic contact around the nanotube. Complex interactions at materials interfaces determine the effective contact resistance.

  • Machine Learning Applied to Multiscale Simulations. A technique for using cached results of quantum mechanical calculations to drive a multiscale simulation of the dynamics of a Si melt. I implemented an efficient method to use a large scale database of atomic configurations using a k-nearest-neighbor approach and ball tree-like search method. Click the text for the journal paper.

  • Performance of a Market Maker Strategy. In programming game Stockfighter, I developed a program to act as a market maker and trade with other participants. Using heuristics about price fluctuations and by quickly cancelling outstanding orders, the algorithm is able to consistently earn a profit in a competitive market under changing conditions. NAV is the net account value ($).

  • Flourish Data Services is the data science company I founded with Kyle Thorsen. We build custom machine learning, reinforcement learning, and business simulation software. We also do web-scraping and build chatbots for twitter and Slack. Check it out!

  • Recipe Amalgamation Made Simple. By scraping over 80,000 recipes from public cooking websites, Gabe Shaughnessy and I assembled a database of popular recipes. We built a custom NLP algorithm to process the recipe names, instructions and descriptions to come up with a so-called metarecipe, describing the essential ingredients for anything you'd like to cook. Is vanilla required for chocolate chip cookies? What the key ingredients in dog food? Is bread really required for bread pudding? Give it a shot and find out! (click the text).
